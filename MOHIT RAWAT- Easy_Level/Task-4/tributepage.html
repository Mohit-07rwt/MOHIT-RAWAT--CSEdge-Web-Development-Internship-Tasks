Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Timeline

  • 1856: Born in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
  • 1884: Emigrated to the United States and worked with Thomas Edison.
  • 1887-1888: Developed the induction motor and patents related to AC power.
  • 1891: Invented the Tesla Coil.
  • 1943: Passed away in New York City.

Quotes

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine."
"If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency and vibration."
